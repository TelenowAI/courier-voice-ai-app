Telenow Courier NDR

When Shiprocket or Delhivery reports a failed delivery (NDR), instantly call the customer with a Telenow AI agent to reschedule or fix the address — then record the outcome.

Telenow connection

Paste your Telenow API key (vai_live_…) from Telenow → Developers → API Keys. We validate it and show your org.

Store & re-attempt

The store name is passed to the agent (e.g. "calling on behalf of …"). Auto re-attempt asks the courier to retry delivery when the customer agrees — requires courier API credentials in your environment.

Courier NDR matchers

Which courier statuses count as a failed delivery (NDR). Comma-separated, case-insensitive substring match. Tune these to match how your courier account labels NDRs.

Recent NDR calls

Failed-delivery events received from your couriers and the calls we placed.

Danger zone

Disconnect this app from Telenow (removes the result webhook subscription) and permanently delete ALL local data: your API key, settings, and the NDR records (which hold customer name & phone). This cannot be undone.
